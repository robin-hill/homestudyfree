Welcome to the Home Study Free website


This website is currently under development and will be developed and improved over time, adding new links to useful websites and Youtube channels on the links page, and expanding the studies page to include a variety of Subjects, Subject Areas, and Subject Sections which will link directly to Video Lectures for those Subject Sections, making it easy for you to find some of the best online content for any given Subject Section.

With thousands of videos out there on Youtube and a huge variety of content provided for free, it's difficult to know wihch is the best content to learn from.... the content varies in quality, and not all content on the internet is necessarily accurate and correct, or easy to follow and understand.

The purpose of this website is to provide a free portal website for students, parents and teachers which acts as a learning hub helping guide you in the direction of quality content which is available for free on the internet. Our aim is also to provide lots of useful links to other websites and to Youtube channels which may help steer you towards useful content and information regarding your studies and regarding homeschool in general.

The studies page lists Subjects of study and provides links to quality content providers on Youtube that you can use to study that subject. Each Subject is broken down into a list of Subject Areas and each Subject Area is broken down into Subject Sections. For each Subject Section approved content providers are listed, together with their Video Lectures for that Subject Section.

The links page provides useful links to websites and Youtube channels which you can visit for your online learning.
